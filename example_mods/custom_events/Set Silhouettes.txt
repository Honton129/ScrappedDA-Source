This event enables Silhouettes of the characters and adds a white bg behind them
like is seen on the "Bad Apple!" video

Value 1: use 'a' to make the BG white and characters black, use 'b' to undo it.
Value 2: Silhouettes Fade In/Out Time. ("0" doesn't work, use 0.01 instead!)
